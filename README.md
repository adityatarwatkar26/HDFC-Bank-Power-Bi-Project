# 🏦 HDFC Bank Loan Analytics Dashboard

A **professional Power BI dashboard project** designed to analyze **loan performance, interest income, NPA losses, and branch-wise financial insights** for HDFC Bank.  
This project demonstrates **data analysis, financial KPI tracking, and business intelligence reporting** using Power BI.

---

# 📌 Project Purpose

The purpose of this project is to **analyze loan data and financial performance of HDFC Bank** through interactive dashboards.

The dashboard helps in:

- Tracking **Interest Income generated from loans**
- Monitoring **Non-Performing Assets (NPA)**
- Analyzing **branch performance**
- Evaluating **profitability and ROI**
- Understanding **loan distribution patterns**

This project showcases how **data analytics can help banks make better financial decisions**.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes the following key insights:

- 📈 **Interest Income Analysis**
- 💰 **Net Profit Calculation**
- ⚠️ **NPA Loss Monitoring**
- 📊 **Loan Distribution by Branch**
- 🧾 **Loan Type Performance**
- 🌍 **Branch Location Map**
- 📉 **Profit Contribution Analysis**
- 🔎 **Decomposition Tree for NPA Analysis**

These visuals help in identifying **high-performing branches, risky loan segments, and overall financial performance**.

## 📊 Key Insights from Retail Loan Portfolio Dashboard

- **Strong Loan Portfolio:** The bank has a **total loan value of 5.05bn across 4,958 transactions**, serving **2,414 customers**, indicating a strong retail lending presence.

- **Stable Loan Disbursement Trend:** Monthly loan disbursement remained relatively **stable between 0.41bn–0.49bn during the first 9 months**, with a noticeable **decline in October–December**, suggesting possible seasonal slowdown or tighter lending.

- **Balanced Risk Distribution:** The **risk categories (Low, Medium, High)** are **almost evenly distributed (~33% each)**, showing a **diversified risk exposure** rather than concentration in a single risk segment.

- **Top Performing Branches:** **Chennai T Nagar and Ahmedabad CG Road** contribute the **highest loan volumes (above 1bn each)**, making them the most impactful branches in the portfolio.

- **Loan Type Concentration:** **Auto loans generate the highest loan amount (~1.06bn)** followed closely by **Education and Business loans**, indicating strong demand in these segments.

- **Moderate Risk Indicator:** The **High Risk Ratio of 33.14%** suggests that **one-third of the loan portfolio carries higher risk**, highlighting the need for careful credit monitoring and risk management.

## 📊 Key Insights from Loan Risk & NPA Monitoring Dashboard

- **High NPA Exposure:** The bank has a **Total NPA Value of 1.69bn** with an **NPA ratio of 33.47%**, indicating that roughly **one-third of the loan portfolio is at risk**, requiring strong credit monitoring.

- **Loan Provision Requirement:** A **loan provision of 253.44M** has been allocated to cover potential losses from non-performing assets, reflecting the bank’s risk mitigation strategy.

- **Peak NPA Months:** The **highest NPA levels occur between May and August (around 170M+)**, while **October to December show a significant decline**, indicating improving loan recovery or reduced defaults.

- **Consistent Risk Across Loan Types:** All major loan types (**Auto, Business, Education, Home, and Personal**) show **similar risk distribution**, meaning risk exposure is spread across products rather than concentrated in a single category.

- **Branch Risk Concentration:** **Mumbai Central branch** shows multiple high NPA entries across loan categories, suggesting it may be a **key contributor to overall NPA risk**.

- **Highest Risk Loan Segment:** **Education loans at Mumbai Central show the highest individual NPA amount (~87.5M)**, indicating that this segment may require **stricter credit assessment or recovery strategies**.



### 📊 Key Insights from Customer Segment & Risk Behaviour

- **Total Customers:** The dataset includes **2,414 customers**, showing a moderately sized customer base for loan analysis.

- **Customer Financial Profile:** The **average loan value is 2.09M**, while the **average salary is 602K**, indicating customers typically take loans significantly higher than their yearly income.

- **Risk Segmentation Trend:** Customers with **higher salaries (~995M)** tend to fall into the **high-risk loan category**, suggesting larger borrowing behavior among high earners.

- **Loan Distribution by Employment:** Loan distribution across **Government, Self-Employed, Salaried, and Business** customers is **almost evenly split (~24–26%)**, indicating balanced lending across employment types.

- **Branch Loan Contribution:** Branches such as **Mumbai Central, Chennai T Nagar, and Ahmedabad CG Road** contribute the **highest loan amounts**, making them key revenue-generating branches.

- **NPA Observation:** The **Total NPA Amount (~1.69B)** is significantly high, highlighting a potential **credit risk concern that may impact overall profitability**.
